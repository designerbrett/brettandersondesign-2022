Energy App Mockup for Funding Presentation

Objectives

The idea for an app that would provide medical facilities information about their energy consumption. How does the facility rank compared to other facilities and what recommendations could be made.

  • RoleLead Designer

  • ToolsSketch & Protopie



My role on this project was to create the style for a shareholder presentation. They wanted a design that was a little more out there (for 2017) and something that visually would be exciting for a presentation. I was able to collaborate with product and engineering leads to get a general idea for the app features and what would be reasonable to accomplish. It was a quick timeline and every limited budget with only 2-3 revisions and meetings before the designs were needed.


The current problem is access to information all in one place with little conversations between other facilities.

The focus was then put on key screens that would provide a quick overview of the information needed to adjust facility resources.

  1. The home screens allow for quick access to 6 key information screens.
  2. A chat feature is also available in the top right. Communication between members and the sustainability liaison group.




The ability to see the current stats for the month and year as a whole.

We focused on the current month view so adjustments could be made, as well as creating a facility comparison screen to see how you rank.

  1. Overview of the facility stats broken up into a current month and year view similar to how you would manage your financial budget.
  2. Health System Overview with similar information to the site overview with additional charts to compare facilities.




Previously its been difficult to examine how a facility is doing compared to last year or similar facilities.

We created graphs that could be filtered and give the manager a sense of how they are tracking by year and by the facility.

  1. Show by default how you are doing each month for the current year with indicators from last year's numbers.
  2. A competition screen is available for managers to set goals and targets associated with similar and/or regional facilities.




An excel sheet of how you are progressing towards your goal is great for details but not for a glance.

Setting goals and showcasing your progress in real-time provides managers with the information they need to keep on top of their planning.

  1. A visual graphic with stats to the sides showcases the progress that is being made and needs to be made to meet the facilities' goals.
  2. Comparing the facility to another is key to setting appropriate goals and pushing your team to be more innovative and better.

Lessons Learned

It was fun to work on such a quick project but provided limited to no research. It's was a challenge to showcase the charts and filters in such a small screen space. It was also difficult to showcase an interactive app within a keynote presentation that I really had no control over how it would be shown at the end of the day.

You can also check out the video here created in ProtoPie and the full prototype >




The desktop version was a lower priority for the group to see. The web app would be pulling from the same database generally displaying the information in the same way. Obviously, with the web version, we have a few more liberties to show more information with additional filters and comparison charts. The colors were adjusted to reduce the amount of blue saturation and keep elements simple.
